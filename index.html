Supernatural Reserve Forces

The S.R.F is a special division of the US Army dedicated to fighting supernatural threats. An international task force, the majority of the SRF’s forces are non-human (approximately 86%) but the SRF often works closely with human units.- The S.R.F has notably different rules and regulations than the human military, particularly regarding fraternisation (which is generally allowed).
This is out of respect for pack dynamics and other relationships typical to supernatural creatures.- In line with this, public intimacy is considered acceptable for supernatural soldiers, due to their biological needs.- There is considerable tension between non-human and human soldiers in the S.R.F.

The Underworld

The supernatural criminal underworld is where those seeking the darker side of magic converge; whether it's purchasing supernatural creatures (sentient or otherwise), smuggling illegal artifacts or finding non-conventional solutions to life's problems, the underworld has something to offer everyone...as long as you're willing to pay the price, at least.The Ballantine Family is one of the better known examples of an organised supernatural mafia, but the underworld has more than its far share of lone wolves - such as smugglers, fixers and treasure hunters.Humans, demihumans and supernatural creatures alike are drawn to the underworld - as long as you've got the means and the drive, you can make it here.

Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of creatures exist in this universe?
> Short answer: basically anything! Demihumans, fairies, vampires, werewolves, orcs, demons, angels, aliens...you name it! The idea of Modern Fantasy is that it's very open and flexible. The "lore" is minimal and creativity is encouraged!


What about robots?
> This is tricky - technology is assumed to be at a 2023-ish level, but magic exists. I generally lean towards "no androids/robots" for my personal worldbuilding.


Do normal animals exist in Modern Fantasy?
> Yes! Normal animals (dogs/cows/horses/birds) all exist, as do fantasy creatures like unicorns, dragons and so on.Can I create a fan oc/use the lore for my own bots?
